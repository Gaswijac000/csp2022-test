Cheesy Spicy pasta Recipe

  • 2 Cups Milk
  • 2 Cans Tomato Paste
  • 1-2 Handfulls Of Cheese Of Your Choice
  • 1 Clove Of Garlic
  • 1 Box Of Farfalle Noodles
  • 1-2 Tablespoons Of Chili Peper Flakes
  • 1 Large Sweet Onion
  1. Buy Supplies, As Seen Above
  2. Get Pot
  3. Fill Pot With Water
  4. Boil Water
  5. While Waiting For Water To Boil, Dice One Large Sweet Onion
  6. Check If Water Is Boiling, If It Is Add Noodles To Pot Cook 10 Minutes
  7. Get Large Pan
  8. Add Oil To Pan
  9. Cook Onions in Pan until Browned
  10. Add One Clove Of Garlic To Pan
  11. Add Tomato Paste To Pan, Stir Well
  12. Slowly Add Milk To Pan, Stir Until Completely Integrated
  13. Add Chili Peper Flakes
  14. Add Noodles
  15. Add Cheese
  16. Serve!
